Services

Three ways to get clarity on your numbers

Pick a starting point based on how much you already have built.

Insight Report

A focused, one-time analysis of your existing data — what's healthy, what's at risk, and three concrete actions to take this month.

  • Delivered as a PDF report
  • Turnaround in 3–5 days
  • Good first step if you're not ready for a full dashboard

Dashboard Build

A living Excel or Power BI dashboard connected to your real data — revenue, retention, channel performance, and product mix, updated by pasting new rows in.

  • Excel or Power BI, your choice
  • Zero hardcoded numbers — every cell traces to source data
  • Includes a 30-minute walkthrough call

Reporting Automation

For businesses ready to stop exporting CSVs by hand. I connect your data sources and schedule a report that lands in your inbox on its own.

  • Ongoing engagement, billed monthly
  • Weekly or monthly refresh, your call
  • Best after an Insight Report or Dashboard Build

About

A solo operator, not an agency of ten Slack channels

Logic Pilot is one person: the same person who scopes the call, builds the dashboard, and answers your follow-up questions. No account manager relaying messages, no junior analyst learning on your data.

The focus is founder-led and small e-commerce businesses that have real data sitting in Shopify, ad platforms, and spreadsheets — but no reliable way to read it as one picture. That's the gap this fills.
